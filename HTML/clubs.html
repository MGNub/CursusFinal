Clubs

Joining a running club or group is one of the best ways to stay motivated. Running with others will allow you to improve greatly, and it just makes running a lot more enjoyable overall. There are dozens of running groups throughout Philadelphia, each different in their own way. With some quick searching, you could find the group that suits your style.

Students Run Philly Style

Students Run Philly Style is a non-profit organization that was founded in 2004. It aims to promote healthy lifestyles, personal achievement, and teamwork among Philadelphia youth through mentorship and long-distance running.

The program provides free mentoring, coaching, and support to students in grades 7-12, with a focus on those who are at risk of dropping out of school or are affected by poverty. Participants are paired with adult mentors who serve as positive role models and help them prepare for and complete a half-marathon or full marathon.

Through the program, students not only gain physical fitness and endurance but also learn important life skills such as goal-setting, perseverance, and leadership. Students Run Philly Style has helped thousands of students to develop healthy habits and achieve personal goals.

Philadelphia Runner

Philadelphia Runner is a running store and community of runners in Philadelphia, PA. The store offers a variety of running shoes, apparel, and accessories from popular brands. In addition to their retail store, Philadelphia Runner also hosts training programs, group runs, and clinics for runners of all levels.

Their training programs include structured workouts and coaching to help runners prepare for races of different distances, from 5Ks to marathons. The group runs are free and open to all, and take place on different days and times throughout the week at various locations in the city.

Philadelphia Runner has several locations throughout the city, including in University City, Center City, and Manayunk. They also have an online store where runners can purchase gear and register for training programs and events. Overall, Philadelphia Runner aims to create a supportive community of runners in Philadelphia and help people achieve their running goals.

Fishtown Beer Runners

The Fishtown Beer Runners is a running group based in Philadelphia, PA. The group was founded in 2007 by David April, who was inspired by the running clubs he saw in Europe that combined exercise and socializing.

Fishtown Beer Runners is a popular running group in Philadelphia, PA. The group meets at 7PM every Thursday at a local bar in the Fishtown neighborhood, runs a 5K route, and then finishes with drinks together at the same bar.

Overall, the Fishtown Beer Runners aim to create a fun and social environment for runners in Philadelphia, where they can enjoy exercise and socializing with like-minded people.

Fairmount Running Club

The Fairmount Running Club is a local running club based in the Fairmount neighborhood of Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. It is a community-driven organization that aims to promote running, fitness, and social connections among its members.

Group runs typically take place in and around the Fairmount neighborhood and may include various distances and paces to accommodate different levels of runners. In addition to running, the club may also engage in other fitness-related activities, as well as social gatherings to foster camaraderie and friendship among its members.

The Fairmount Running Club is a grassroots organization that relies on its members to volunteer and contribute to the club's activities and initiatives. It is a popular running club in the Philadelphia area and has built a community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for running and an active lifestyle.
