Running in Philly

Running is a sport that has existed since the beginning of time. You'll find many runners in Philadelphia, a city with dozens of beautiful running locations, and a strong community of runners dedicated to a healthy lifestyle. Through running, many people have transformed their lives for the better, and you could meet some of the best people out there.

How Should I Start?

Everyone starts somewhere, and this could be where you start your journey. It's important to be consistent with your running and stay motivated. Running with others is the best way to get motivated so it is recommended you find people or friends to run with. Finding a nice place to run to and appreciate can also help boost your motivation.

Remember to stay hydrated! It is important to stay hydrated while running. Make sure you drink enough water before, during, and after your run.

Warm up before running: Before you start running, it is important to warm up your muscles. You can do this by doing some light exercises like walking or jogging slowly.

Wearing the right clothing and shoes can make a big difference in your comfort level while running. Make sure you have comfortable and breathable clothing and proper running shoes.

Remember to follow common running etiquette, such as obeying traffic laws, being mindful of other people, and clean up after yourself. Also make sure to keep your headphones volume low or keep one ear out so you can hear your surroundings.
