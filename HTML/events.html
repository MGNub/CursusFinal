Races

Participating in races is a great way to get more involved with the competitive running scene. It allows you to set goals, measure your growth, and connect with the community. Many races are also organized to raise money for charity, allowing runners to support a cause they believe in.

There are many races in the city of Philadelphia every year, so feel free to check them out, and maybe even sign up for one!
